Topic 3

The migration journey

Applicaa prepares (behind the scenes)

  • Enable the integration
  • Check and update your forms
  • Map your address lines
  • Generate a review list of values to check

You do (with our guidance)

  • Install the app and import your lookups
  • Map your lookup values
  • Run a test transfer to confirm
Standard school up to 14 days
MAT up to 30 days
Avoid enrolment week
Topic 3 (continued)

Topic 4

Data Export Process

Sending an applicant from Admissions+ into Civica

Open Import Applications
this is the "export", A+ to Civica
Choose the form and a completed applicant
Pick the fields to send
Confirm, watch the live progress report
The app creates or updates the record in Civica and returns the student number
The app creates new records and updates existing ones in Civica. The Civica student number it returns confirms the write-back worked.
